# Northstar: FIFA World Cup 2026 Smart Stadium Platform

Northstar is the definitive AI-native matchday operating system, designed explicitly to solve the FIFA Smart Stadium challenge. Built for the FIFA World Cup 2026, Northstar utilizes real-time operational intelligence, predictive analytics, and Google Gemini Gen AI to revolutionize stadium operations, crowd management, and fan safety.

## Core Pillars & Problem Statement Alignment

Northstar directly addresses the core requirements of a smart, safe, and efficient stadium environment:

- **Fan Safety & Crowd Management**: Real-time telemetry, spatial mapping, and predictive analytics prevent crowd crushes and optimize ingress/egress.
- **Stadium Operations & Tournament Management**: The Staff and Organizer views provide an omniscient digital twin of the stadium for rapid resource allocation.
- **Volunteer Coordination & Staff Productivity**: AI Copilots route volunteers to critical areas, prioritize maintenance tasks, and offer live multilingual translation.
- **AI-Assisted Decision Making & Emergency Response**: Google Gemini processes live telemetry to generate actionable operational recommendations and emergency routing.
- **Smart Navigation**: Optimized pathfinding algorithms direct fans to less congested gates, restrooms, and concessions.
- **Accessibility & Sustainability**: Designed with WCAG 2.1 AA compliance and optimized digital infrastructure for maximum efficiency.

## Key Features

1. **Role-Based Digital Twin**: Tailored interfaces for Fans, Volunteers, Staff, and Organizers.
2. **Gemini Operations Copilot**: Conversational AI that understands live stadium telemetry (incidents, weather, crowd flow).
3. **Real-time Operational Intelligence**: Live heatmaps, transit hub tracking, and predictive incident management.
4. **Multilingual Support**: Instantaneous communication and translation for a global fan base.

## Technology Stack
- Next.js 15 (App Router)
- React 19 & Framer Motion
- Google Generative AI (Gemini 1.5 Flash)
- Zustand (State Management)
- Tailwind CSS

## Deployment
Deployed on Vercel for edge-optimized performance and maximum reliability during high-traffic tournament events.
